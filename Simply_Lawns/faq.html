FAQs


Why do you mow my lawn when it is wet?

We try to mow so that we don't miss a week.

Do you do landscape design and/or planting?

Simply put we do not normally do landscape design. We will be willing to plant almost anything given that you give us a heads up, so that we may research what you have us plant.

What if you miss our scheduled date?

If we were to miss our scheduled date due to heavy rain or anything else, we might come back the next day or we might come back the next week (and not charge you).

What if I were to cancel services from you?

We will stop all services and payments upon cancellation.

What should I do with my pets on the day that you are set to mow?

You should make sure that they are inside of the house on the day that we are set to come. We will let you know the time that we will be at your home.

How do you determine the price?

We determine price by the size of the yard, and how long it takes for us to mow. When determining the price the first test mow is free, and you can decide if you would like to continue with services after the first mow.
