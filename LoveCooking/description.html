What is Cooking?


Cooking is the ability to turn a set of ingredients into a variety of meals with the help of a recipe.
The food that you cook can be broken down into subgroups as there are numerous types of food, certainly
true when you account for the food from other cultures. You can use the same ingredients to make
different kinds of food.
